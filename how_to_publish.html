How to upload a website online

On this page I will show you instructions on how to upload a webpage or website to a server so you can access it from anywhere.

Getting started

First you will need to download a FTP(file transfer protocol) manager. I prefer to use filezilla it's free and easy to use.

For this example we will be using the Icarus web server provided by Weber State to students learning web developement. You will need a protocol(hostname), username, password, and port number to upload to the server.

After you have connected to the server you can do one of two things, you can either upload your files one at a time

or all at once by dragging and dropping your folder.

Once you have uploaded your files go to the URL and verify that your files are working correctly.
