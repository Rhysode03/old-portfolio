URL examples

There are three ways you can link sites together once you have published your site. The following are examples of each.

You will need a couple different items to get to your uploaded files. The first is the protocol: https://. This will let the web know what kind of connection to make. The next is your host name: icarus.cs.weber.edu/. After that you'll need your username: ~kc97637. Then the path to your files: subfolder. And the last thing you'll need in your URL is your filename: index.html.

URL with the .html

https://icarus.cs.weber.edu/~kc97637/hw2/index.html

URL with a secondary folder

https://icarus.cs.weber.edu/~kc97637/hw2/url_examples.html

URL without file name in the URL

https://icarus.cs.weber.edu/~kc97637/hw2/
